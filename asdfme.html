About Me

I've always has a passion for photography and gadgets. Using a modern drone for Aerial photography allows me to indulge both of these. I've been flying for 2 years ever since I tried one and just had to get my own. I truly enjoy the new perspective that I can get, whether it's just a little over head or 400 feet in the sky!

I have recently moved to Denver from Santa Barbara, California. I'm loving the active lifestyle that is so prevalent here. I've been loving the biking around here and am looking forward to hitting the slopes when winter rolls around! I'm also learning web and graphic design.
