About Rocky Sky Photo

Rocky Sky is a one man shop consisting of me, Sam Selfridge. I'm fully licened to fly commercially through FAA part 107 and I'm insured up to $1,000,000.

I've always has a passion for photography and gadgets. Using a drone for aerial photography allows me to indulge both. I've been flying for 2 years ever since I tried one and just had to get my own. I truly enjoy the new perspective that I can get, whether it's just a little over head or 400 feet in the sky!

One of my favorite things about modern drones is their ability to utilize GPS techonology to not only making flying easier, but to enable the pilot to fly the same paths multiple days. That combined with the 4k video makes this the most exciting digital work I've been able to do!

My hobbies include cycling and riding my motorcycle, and my snowboard when the snow arrives! I'm also learning web development and graphic design. This entire website was built from scratch by me!
